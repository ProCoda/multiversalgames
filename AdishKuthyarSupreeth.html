Quantum

Computer

Do you ever feel like the innovative part of technology has come to a stop?

Embark on the thrilling adventure in the Quantum realm. Quantum Computer is a game where education and fun syncs well together. This game makes the player journey to every corner of the world from mountains to desert and to the city in search of the stolen Quantum Computer parts. A journey with limitless possibilities, A new type of technology and the reward being an in game Quantum Computer.

Adventurous plot

Map navigation

The map allows you to navigate through the games realms easily and quickily. Just clicking on the M key will take you to the map mode where you can click on the realm you want to travel to. The quick access to the 5 realms of the quantumverse with a single click makes the game navigatable and adventurous.

Mini Games

This game offers players the opportunity to play 3 different types of games that test you in 3 different areas:

  • Memory Game: Tests you on your ability to remember the card symbols by matching them.
  • Sliding Puzzle: Tests you on your ability to use your brain to solve a puzzle on time
  • Dodge the fireballs: Tests you on your speed and reaction time.

These mini games makes the whole game experience fun and less repetitive.

Educational Content

This game as well as being fun is also an educational game. As the games name is titled, this game educates the player about the quantum computer by adding answers and at the completion of the game more information on the game.

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys to move the Centaur while playing the dodging game.
  • Press on the puzzle pieces so that the puzzle slides to the open space.
  • Press on the cards so that it faces up.
